Our Memberships

BeyondLanding offers three types of memberships ­— Program, VJ25 and Corporate.

All memberships are investment free and come with a range of exclusive benefits including access to Private World and VistaJet’s iconic onboard services.

Each membership can be customized to your needs upon joining, with flexibility in your contract to access the Members' fleet of 360 aircraft, including requesting a specific aircraft type based on your flight mission.
